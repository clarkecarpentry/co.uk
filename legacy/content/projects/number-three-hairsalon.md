# Number Three Hair Salon

**URL:** /no-3-hairsalon
**Type:** Commercial - Bespoke Joinery
**Location:** Bath

## Description

Clarke Carpentry Contractors Ltd were awarded the contract to design, build and install a new bespoke mirror for this renowned hair salon in the heart of Bath. The salon had undergone a redesign, changing the upstairs area from a flat to a commercial studio. The project was delivered on budget and on time.

**Work Completed:** November 2017
