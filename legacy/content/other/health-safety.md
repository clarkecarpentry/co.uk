# Health & Safety

**URL:** /health-safety

## Overview

Having third party accreditation from construction industry specialists, Clarke Carpentry Contractors Ltd aim to be fully compliant with health and safety laws and provide safe working environments for its staff and clients.

**Head Carpenter Qualification:** Level 3 NVQ in Site Carpentry and Joinery

All carpenters are CSCS approved and supervised by forepersons with either SMSTS or SSSTS.

## Accreditations

### SSSTS - Site Supervisor Safety Training Scheme

This certificate is for our site supervisors and provides an understanding of health, safety, welfare and environmental issues on construction sites.

### CSCS - Construction Skills Certification Scheme

CSCS cards provide proof that individuals working on construction sites have the appropriate training and qualifications for the job they do on site. All of our carpenters hold an up to date card.

- [CSCS Website](https://www.cscs.uk.com/)

### SMSTS - Site Management Safety Training Scheme

This certificate is for our site managers and supervisors and provides an understanding of health, safety, welfare and environmental issues on construction sites.

- [CITB SMSTS Course](https://www.citb.co.uk/national-construction-college/health-and-safety-courses/site-management-safety-training-scheme-smsts/)

### PASMA - Prefabricated Access Suppliers' and Manufacturers' Association

PASMA is the recognized focus and authority for mobile access towers. PASMA advances safety, standards and best practice across a wide range of sectors.

- [PASMA Website](https://pasma.co.uk/)
